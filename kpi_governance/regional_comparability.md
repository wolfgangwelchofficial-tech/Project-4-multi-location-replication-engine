# KPI Tolerance Enforcement Model

Standardization without enforcement creates drift.

---

## Enforcement Structure

| KPI                     | Target | Green Band | Yellow Band | Red Band |
|-------------------------|--------|------------|------------|----------|
| Labor % of Revenue      | 28%    | ±3%        | ±6%        | >6%      |
| Gross Margin            | 65%    | ±2%        | ±5%        | >5%      |
| Revenue per FTE         | $12k   | ±5%        | ±10%       | >10%     |
| Inventory Turnover      | 4x     | ±0.5       | ±1.0       | >1.0     |

Locations in Red triggered mandatory intervention review.
