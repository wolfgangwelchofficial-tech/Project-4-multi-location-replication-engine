# KPI Harmonization Framework

Expansion creates metric drift.

To enable regional comparability, KPIs were standardized across markets.

---

## Harmonization Controls

- Unified revenue categorization.
- Margin normalization logic.
- Labor-to-revenue ratio thresholds.
- Inventory velocity benchmarks.
- Weekly performance dashboards.

---
